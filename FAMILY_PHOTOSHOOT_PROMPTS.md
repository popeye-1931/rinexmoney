# Family Photoshoot Prompt Pack

Use each person's real uploaded image as the identity reference.

## Global Direction

- Keep the face exactly identical to the uploaded reference image.
- Do not change facial identity, age, skin tone, facial proportions, eye shape, nose shape, lips, hairline, or facial hair pattern.
- Keep the series visually consistent across all members.
- Use the same studio setup or a very similar premium neutral background in every shot.
- Use soft diffused studio lighting, clean shadows, high detail skin texture, premium portrait finish, natural color grading, 4k quality.
- Keep pose changes subtle only.
- Keep styling modest, traditional, and culturally appropriate.
- Use a 4:5 portrait composition, studio portrait look, premium editorial family-photoshoot aesthetic.

## Shared Style Lock

Use this in every prompt:

`studio portrait, soft diffused lighting, elegant neutral background in warm beige or taupe, premium clean aesthetic, high detail, realistic skin texture, sharp eyes, natural expression, subtle pose variation only, traditional South Asian Muslim family portrait style, 85mm lens look, shallow depth of field, 4k, ultra detailed, realistic photography`

## Negative Prompt

Use this in every generation:

`different face, different person, altered identity, beauty filter, face swap, de-aging, age change, skin tone change, unrealistic skin, plastic skin, extra fingers, distorted hands, warped clothing, asymmetrical eyes, wrong facial hair, oversharpened skin, low resolution, noisy image, messy background, harsh flash, dramatic cinematic lighting, exaggerated pose, fashion runway pose, western outfit, casual t-shirt, duplicate person`

For Rida also add:

`adult makeup, mature styling, glamorous pose, revealing outfit`

- Male members:
  - Beige kurta pajama
  - Black Pakistani-style kurta pajama

  - Light pastel casual kurta
- Female members:
  - Beige modest traditional suit
  - Black Pakistani suit with dupatta
  - Festive embroidered outfit
  - Light pastel casual kurta or suit with dupatta
- Keep each person's modest style aligned with their current appearance in the reference photo.
